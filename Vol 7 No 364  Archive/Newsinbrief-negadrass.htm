Business News In Brief  From Addis Zemen

Cement

Over 95, 000tns of Cement to be Imported
 

In the coming two weeks, over 95,000tns of cement will be imported. According to information from Ministry of Trade and Industry, 23 importers have begun supplying cement for the market as well as for own consumption. Though the price of Mugher Cement is hiking, imported foreign cement is stabilizing the price in the market.

 

 

 

(Source: Negadras, January 9, 2007)

 

Abyssinia

Abyssinia Share Co. est. in S. Africa
 

Ten Ethiopians residing in South Africa established a share company named Abyssinia Share Company. The company will set out on real estate development, hotel, and tourism. The capital of the company is expected to expand when Ethiopians in South Africa begin to buy shares. Sources disclosed that 50 to 70 thousand Ethiopians reside in South Africa. These Ethiopians have instituted Ethiopian Chamber of Commerce in South Africa before eight months, which opens opportunities for Ethiopian made textile and other products to penetrate South African markets.

 

 

(Source: Negadras, January 9, 2007)
