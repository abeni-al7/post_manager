Indian Investments in Ethiopia on the Rise

Indian private companies are increasingly looking toward Ethiopia for investments and joint ventures. Till January 16, 2007, 237 private Indian companies have obtained project approvals from the Ethiopian Investment Authority, with a total approved capital of $ 427 million. Twenty-six of these companies have more than $ 5 million capital each, while 90 companies have capital $ 1 million each. Many of these are in operation or at a project implementation stage. These cover different sectors like agriculture and floriculture, engineering, plastics, consultancy and ICT, water management, cotton and textile, education, hotel and restaurant services, vehicle rentals, pharmaceuticals and health care.

 

Source: The Reporter, January 27, 2007
