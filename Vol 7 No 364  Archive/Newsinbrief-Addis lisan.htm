Kirkos Kifle Ketema Collects 81m Br

 

Bureau of Revenue of kirkos kifle ketema disclosed that if has collected over 81 million Br form various revenue sources during the first three months of the current budget year. The district collected 101pc of its plan that was to collect 99 million Br during this budget year. The amount of revenue obtained from various taxes has shown a 6, 744,261 Br increase from last year.

 

 

(Source: Addis Lisan October 21, 2006)
