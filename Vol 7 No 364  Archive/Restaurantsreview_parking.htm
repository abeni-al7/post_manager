Parking      

An enclosed area is provided, except that it will probably not accommodate more than three cars at a time, without the need for diners to move back and forth to let vehicles out, in which case the accommodation size can rise to five cars.
 

However customers can park on the side of the asphalt road, which is more convenient. As there is security provided, people should not really worry about their cars. If taking a cab or walking, the place is close enough from Africa Avenue, making it a convenient location.
