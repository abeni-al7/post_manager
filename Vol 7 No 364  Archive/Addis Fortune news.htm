Sudanese Company to Manufacture Security Doors in Addis
 

BESFOUR Khartoum, a Sudanese manufacturer of security doors and windows, prefabs, kitchen materials and furniture, is to start production in Ethiopia with a capital of 10 million Br.

Of these, 75, with capital of 1.1 billion Br, are currently constructing their facilities, while 308 companies, accounting for a total capital of 2.3 billion Br, have already started operation.
