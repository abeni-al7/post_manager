Price      

When looking at the menu, the prices at JOI appear fair. The prices for main courses range from 25 Br for a lentil dish to 75 Br for prawn dishes. Nevertheless, when the receipt is placed on the table, one will for some reason be surprised at how much they have to pay.
 

True enough, 15pc VAT and 10pc service charge are included and beverages like Ambo do go for six Birr; nevertheless, although customers might be surprised at the amount they pay in the end, the prices still remain fair enough, considering the quality of the food and the portions.
