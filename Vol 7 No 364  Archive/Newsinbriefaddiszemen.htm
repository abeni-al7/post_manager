Exports to Israel Generate 26.3M Dollars

 

The Israeli Embassy said that Ethiopia obtained over 26.3 million dollars from agricultural and industrial products it exported to Israel last year. The trade exchange between the two countries stands at over 36 million dollars. Isreali Ambassador, Yaacov Amitai, said imports from Israel accounted for 10.4 million dollars. He said the investment and trade relations between the two countries haveconsiderably improved in recent years. The Ambassador also said the two countries are currently enjoying excellent government to government and people to people relations, more than ever before.

 

Source: Addis Zemen, March 27, 2007
