Ethiopian Leather Industry Leaders Make Russian Connections
 

Ethiopian tanners and leather companies returned to Addis Abeba this week from a successful trade delegation to Moscow. The four person delegation returned with a substantial initial trial order for four containers of wet blue, a semi-finished hide, and one container of dyed and natural crust hides. Apart from commercial negotiations between Ethiopian tannery and leather exporters and Russian buyers, a memorandum of understanding was also reached between the Ethiopian Leather Industry Association and the Russian Tannery and Footwear Union at a series of meetings in Moscow.  The delegation also visited the largest tannery in Europe, a few hours southeast of Moscow, where they have a capacity to tan up to 8,000 hides a day.
 

Source: Press Release, March 15, 2007
