Sanitation      

As the restaurant is set in the villa, both the ladies’ and men’s rooms both have a washbasin with a mirror above and working taps provided. Both have liquid soap provided on the basin, automatic hand dryers and unplugged automatic liquid soap dispenser machines. Both restrooms have ample toilet paper, flush that work and are free of stench. Despite the fact that there are dustbins and doors that lock, the walls as well as basin in the men’s room were not clean to the eye.

 

Despite the fact that it looked like not too much effort had been made to personalize the restrooms. They were both within a good distance of each other, had marked doors and hand dryers.
