DBE to Begin New Loan System
     
 

 

The executive management of the Development Bank of Ethiopia (DBE) decided to begin implementing a collateral management loan system.
 

The latest system would inject working capital into manufacturing industries that have been foreclosed on by the Bank. The currently idle businesses would have professional management for their financial department hired by the Bank.

Economic Feature-Ethiopian Development Discourse in Historical Perspective
     
 

Ethiopia's pioneer development economists created advanced discourses on the problems of growth. An analysis of their past writings shows that many of the ideas bare direct relevance on today's dilemmas. Alemayehu Geda (PhD), Associate Professor of Economics at Addis Abeba University, argues that current policy makers and academics must take heed of these historical works in order to conjure insightful recommendations for the coming century.

Movie Review-Maheder
     
 

Maheder, the latest Amharic feature film, premiered Monday, April 16, 2007 at the National Theatre. After a two hour technical delay, actor Kiros Haile Selassie's introduction and apology on behalf of Wabe Films was greeted with a grand applause from the packed theatre.
 

A heavily plot driven suspense, Maheder revolves around the conflict between pursuing happiness in the present and digging through one's past, seeking a truth that may hurt everyone involved. The opening scene finds Abraham (Henok Berta), a young artist, and Winta (Mahelet Mulugeta), in a sweeping romance happily devoid of problems.
